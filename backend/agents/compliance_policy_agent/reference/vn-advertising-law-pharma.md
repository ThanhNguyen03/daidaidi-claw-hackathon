# Vietnamese Advertising Law — Pharma & FMCG Reference

Sources:
- Luật Quảng cáo số 16/2012/QH13 (amended 2025)
- Nghị định 181/2013/NĐ-CP
- Nghị định 70/2021/NĐ-CP (amendment)
- Nghị định 38/2021/NĐ-CP (penalties)
- Thông tư 09/2015/TT-BYT (pharma/TPCN ad content approval)
- Luật Dược số 105/2016/QH13

---

## 1. General Advertising Rules (All Products)

- All advertising claims must be **truthful, accurate, and verifiable**
- Cannot use superlatives that cannot be substantiated: "tốt nhất", "số 1", "duy nhất" without evidence
- Cannot use testimonials from individuals not genuinely using the product
- Cannot create false urgency or scarcity
- Cannot imply government endorsement without authorization

---

## 2. Pharmaceuticals (Thuốc)

### Prescription drugs (thuốc kê đơn)
- **Advertising to the general public is PROHIBITED**
- May only be communicated to healthcare professionals (HCP) in approved channels
- Exception: public health campaigns authorized by the Ministry of Health

### OTC drugs (thuốc không kê đơn)
- Advertising allowed but requires **Cục Quản lý Dược** approval of ad content
- Must include: generic name, indications, contraindications, side effects summary
- Cannot claim to cure diseases not in the approved indication list
- Must include: *"Đọc kỹ hướng dẫn sử dụng trước khi dùng"*

### Absolute prohibitions for all drugs
- Claims of complete cure ("chữa khỏi hoàn toàn")
- Claims of zero side effects
- Before/after imagery implying guaranteed results
- Use of children in drug ads without Ministry of Health approval

---

## 3. Food Supplements / TPCN (Thực phẩm Chức năng)

### Required approvals
- Client must hold **Cục ATTP (An toàn Thực phẩm)** approval certificate
- Ad content must match approved product dossier exactly
- Any change to claims requires new approval

### Mandatory disclaimer (bắt buộc in mọi quảng cáo TPCN)
> **"Thực phẩm này không phải là thuốc, không có tác dụng thay thế thuốc chữa bệnh"**

This disclaimer must be:
- Legible (font size not smaller than body text)
- Present in all formats: video, banner, message, OA post

### Prohibited for TPCN
- Words: "chữa", "điều trị", "đặc trị", "thần dược", "chữa khỏi", "100% hiệu quả"
- Using images or names of doctors, hospitals, or medical institutions to imply medical endorsement
- Claims that the product is clinically proven without published, peer-reviewed evidence
- Before/after health transformation claims
- Implying the product treats specific diseases

### Common TPCN allowed claim patterns
| Instead of... | Use... |
|---|---|
| "Chữa đau khớp" | "Hỗ trợ giảm triệu chứng đau khớp" |
| "Điều trị tiểu đường" | "Hỗ trợ ổn định đường huyết" |
| "Tăng cường miễn dịch tuyệt đối" | "Hỗ trợ tăng cường sức đề kháng" |
| "Giảm cân nhanh trong 7 ngày" | "Hỗ trợ quản lý cân nặng" |

## 4. FMCG — Food & Beverage

- Health claims for regular food products require scientific substantiation
- Cannot claim to prevent or treat diseases
- Nutritional claims (high protein, low fat) must meet Ministry of Health standards
- Natural/organic claims must be certified

### Infant & Young Child Nutrition — Strictest Rules
- **PROHIBITED**: advertising breast milk substitutes for children **under 24 months**
- **PROHIBITED**: advertising complementary foods for infants **under 6 months**
- Baby formula ads must not suggest superiority to breast milk
- Violations carry criminal liability under Nghị định 15/2018/NĐ-CP

---

## 5. Cosmetics & Personal Care

- Cannot claim medical effects (treating skin diseases, acne medication)
- "Dermatologist tested" claims must be substantiated
- SPF claims require testing certification
- Cannot use before/after skin condition imagery suggesting disease treatment

---

## 6. Penalties (Nghị định 38/2021 + Luật Quảng cáo 2025 amendments)

| Violation | Administrative Fine |
|---|---|
| False advertising claims | 60–80 million VND |
| TPCN without mandatory disclaimer | 20–30 million VND |
| Advertising prescription drugs to public | 80–100 million VND |
| Infant formula violation | 80–100 million VND + product recall |
| Repeat violations | Up to 2x fine + ad suspension |
| Serious cases | Criminal prosecution under Điều 197 BLHS |

---

## 7. Documentation Checklist for Sales Team

Before pitching pharma/FMCG campaigns, request from client:

- [ ] Cục ATTP certificate (for TPCN)
- [ ] Cục Quản lý Dược ad approval (for OTC drugs)
- [ ] Product registration number (số đăng ký)
- [ ] Approved product dossier (hồ sơ công bố sản phẩm)
- [ ] Scientific evidence for any specific health claims
- [ ] Certification for nutritional/organic claims (if applicable)
