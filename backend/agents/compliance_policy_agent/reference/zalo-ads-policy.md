# Zalo Ads & ZBS Policy Reference

Source: ads.zalo.me + zalo.solutions (official) — Last verified: June 2026

---

## 1. General Principles

- Advertisers are **fully responsible** for ensuring their ads comply with Vietnamese law
- Zalo Ads reserves the right to **reject or remove any ad** without providing a reason
- Zalo Ads does **not** use sensitive personal data for targeting
- Any data collected via Zalo Ads tools (forms, ZNS) requires **user consent**
- Zalo Ads policies apply to all formats: Display, Message Ads, Form Ads, Commerce Ads

---

## 2. Ad Formats Overview

| Format | Use Case | Key Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Display Ads | Brand awareness, reach | Standard content policy applies |
| Message Ads | Direct to OA chat | Requires verified OA |
| Form Ads (ZNS Form) | Lead generation, data collection | Consent notice mandatory |
| Commerce Ads | Product sales | Requires product documentation |
| ZNS | Transactional notifications | Cannot be used for promotions |

---

## 3. Prohibited Content (All Formats)

- False, misleading, or unverifiable claims
- Content that violates Vietnamese law or Zalo community standards
- Discriminatory content based on race, religion, gender, disability
- Gambling, lotteries not licensed by the state
- Counterfeit goods or services
- Political advertising without authorization
- Content targeting minors inappropriately
- Tobacco and e-cigarettes
- Pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing without license

---

## 4. Licensed / Restricted Product Categories

Products in the following categories **require documentation** before ads can run:

| Category | Required Documentation |
|---|---|
| Pharmaceuticals (thuốc) | Cục Quản lý Dược approval + ad content approval |
| Food supplements / TPCN | Cục ATTP (Cục An toàn Thực phẩm) approval certificate |
| Financial services | License from State Bank of Vietnam (SBV) or SSC |
| Real estate | Business registration + project legal documents |
| Insurance | Insurance business license |
| Medical devices | DMEC (Cục Quản lý Dược) registration |
| Education | Ministry of Education recognition (for degree programs) |
| Alcohol | License; cannot target users under 18 |

---

## 5. ZNS (Zalo Notification Service) — Critical Rules

ZNS is a **transactional channel only**. Misuse is a common compliance failure.

**Allowed ZNS uses:**
- Order confirmation, shipping updates, appointment reminders
- OTP / authentication messages
- Service status notifications (payment success, account changes)
- Post-service feedback requests

**NOT allowed via ZNS:**
- Promotional offers, discounts, new product announcements
- Marketing campaigns disguised as service notifications
- Broadcast to users who have not interacted with the service

**Penalty for misuse:** OA suspension + ZNS access revocation

---

## 6. Data Collection via Ads

When using Form Ads or any ad format that collects personal data:
- Consent notice must appear **before** data is collected
- Purpose of collection must be stated clearly
- Data may only be used for the stated purpose
- Must comply with PDPL 2025 + NĐ 13/2023 (see `vn-data-privacy.md`)

---

## 7. ZBS (Zalo Business Solutions) — Account Tiers

ZBS bundles OA + Mini App + ZNS + Ads into tiered service plans.
Compliance obligations apply across all components regardless of tier.
Higher tiers unlock more features but carry the same (or stricter) policy requirements.
