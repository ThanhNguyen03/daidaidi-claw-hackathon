# Discount Policy

## Maximum Discounts

- **Standard Maximum**: 20% off list price
- **Strategic Accounts**: Up to 25% with VP approval
- **Competitor Transitions**: Case-by-case, requires CEO approval

## Approval Requirements

| Discount Level | Approver |
|---------------|----------|
| 0-10% | Sales Rep |
| 10-15% | Sales Manager |
| 15-20% | VP Sales |
| 20%+ | CEO |

## Competitor Client Policy

- Discounts to clients switching from competitors require additional justification
- Must document competitor name and contract value
- Maximum 15% without CEO approval

## Prohibited Discounts

- Discounts tied to future business promises
- Unofficial/off-record discounts
- Discounts to government entities above 10% (compliance issues)

## Documentation

All discounts above 10% must include:
1. Written approval email
2. Business justification
3. Expected contract value
