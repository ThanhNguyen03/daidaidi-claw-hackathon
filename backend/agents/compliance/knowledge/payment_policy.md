# Payment Terms Policy

## Advance Payment

- **Minimum Advance**: 30% of total contract value
- **Custom Development**: 50% advance required
- **Hardware/Software Purchase**: 100% advance

## Credit Terms

- **Standard Credit**: Net 30 days
- **Extended Credit**: Up to 90 days with CFO approval
- **Credit Check Required**: For credit > 30 days and value > 100M VND

## Payment Methods

Accepted:
- Bank Transfer (preferred)
- Cheque
- Credit Card (with 2.5% surcharge)

Not Accepted:
- Cash (except small purchases < 5M VND)
- IOUs

## Installment Plans

- Available for contracts > 500M VND
- Maximum 4 installments
- First payment at signing, last within 12 months
- Interest may apply (CFO approval required)

## Late Payments

- 10% penalty per month on overdue amounts
- Service suspension after 60 days overdue
- Legal action after 90 days

## Refund Policy

- Full refund if cancelled before work begins
- Partial refund based on work completed
- No refund after delivery for custom work
