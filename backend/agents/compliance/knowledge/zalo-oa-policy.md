# Zalo OA Policy Reference

Source: oa.zalo.me (official) — Last verified: June 2026

---

## 1. Account Registration & Naming

### Naming Rules
OA name must follow: **[Prefix] + [Main Name] + [Suffix]**

Main name must be one of:
1. Legal business name / store name / branch name
2. Trademark with IP protection certificate from NOIP (Cục Sở hữu trí tuệ)
3. Other names — subject to conditional approval (regulated industries)

Prefix/suffix: descriptive words for industry, service type, location, branch.

### Verification (Xác thực)
- Admin must submit verification documents within **14 days** of account creation
- Processing time: **2–3 business days**
- If rejected: admin may resubmit within 14 days; after that → **permanent account lock**
- Verification methods: by business name, by registered trademark, or by other name (conditional)

### Account Update Policy
Every edit to name, bio, avatar, cover image, or custom URL goes through re-review.

---

## 2. Content Rules (Nội dung truyền đạt)

The OA owner is **fully responsible** for all content communicated to end users.

### Required
- Maps of Vietnam must correctly represent national sovereignty
- All links must be lawful, clear, and not misleading
- Native app links must belong to the official Zalo ecosystem only

### Prohibited Content
- Links to websites with illegal content
- Gruesome, offensive, or culturally inappropriate images
- Images depicting smoking, drug use, addictive substances
- Superstitious content (mê tín dị đoan)
- False or defamatory information about organizations or individuals
- Copyright-infringing content or content banned by state authorities
- Anti-state propaganda; undermining national unity
- Incitement to war, ethnic hatred, or violence
- Historical distortion; insulting national heroes
- Any other content prohibited by Vietnamese law

## 3. Messaging Policy

### Message Types Summary
| Type | Purpose | Quota |
|---|---|---|
| Broadcast (tin chủ động) | Marketing, promotions | See limits below |
| Response message (tin phản hồi) | Reply to user-initiated chat | 8 free/48h window |
| ZNS (Zalo Notification Service) | Transactional notifications | Per ZBS contract |

### Broadcast Limits
- **Unverified OA**: 20 messages/month total; 1 message/month per follower
- **Verified OA**: 40 messages/month total; 4 messages/month per follower
- **Transactional cap**: max **1 marketing message/day** and **30/month** per individual follower

### Response Message Window
- 8 free response messages per 48-hour window after user sends a message
- After 8: message #9 counts as a proactive (paid) message for verified OAs; unverified OAs cannot send further

### Prohibited Messaging Behaviors
1. Offensive, sensitive, or illegal content in messages
2. Irrelevant frequency/context — messages unrelated to the user's needs
3. False, unverified, or misleading information
4. Impersonation of individuals or organizations; fraud or deception
5. Misuse of message types (e.g., using transactional ZNS for promotions)

### Violation Handling
Zalo monitors bad-report (báo xấu) rates. Escalating tiers over a **12-week rolling window**:
- Warning → Feature restriction → Temporary lock → Permanent lock
- Severe violations (data leaks, serious fraud): immediate permanent lock without warning

---

## 4. Account Lock Policy

Zalo may suspend or terminate OA service for:
- Non-compliance with any policy above
- Negative impact on end-user experience
- Fraudulent or abusive behaviors
- Severe platform violations (data breaches, systematic fraud, harassment)

---

## 5. Quick Compliance Checklist for OA Content

- [ ] Message content is relevant to the follower's relationship with the OA
- [ ] Broadcast frequency ≤ 1/day and ≤ 30/month per follower
- [ ] No prohibited words or imagery
- [ ] All links are legal and lead to expected destinations
- [ ] No impersonation or false claims about products/services
- [ ] Verified OA (required for paid features)
- [ ] Response messages used within the 48h window only
