\# AdtimaBox Case Study Advisor



\*\*Scope:\*\* When sales inputs a client brief or prospect profile, match it to the most relevant case study and recommend a solution direction.



\*\*Output always includes:\*\*

1\. Matched case(s) with alias and rationale for the match

2\. Recommended solution: CShub package + Campaign instant add-ons

3\. Key selling point / proof point from the matched case

4\. Refer to `adtimabox-product-advisor` for detailed pricing and feature specs



\*\*Does NOT do:\*\* Answer pricing questions, explain feature details, or quote packages — that is the product advisor's job.



\---

\*\*CS-12 — Global pharma MNC, HCP-facing, new product MCE launch + Salesforce-integrated HCP Hub\*\*

\- Industry: Pharma / Healthcare — B2B HCP

\- Target: B2B — doctors (endocrinologist, cardiologist, nephrologist), segmented by hospital & specialty

\- Channel: MedRep, offline/online medical events, email marketing, Zalo OA + MiniApp

\- Pain point: New product launch requires building an HCP database from scratch, onboarding across specialties onto one digital hub, personalizing engagement per specialty, and syncing with existing enterprise Salesforce CRM

\- Solution: HCP Community Hub on Zalo (Zalo OA + MiniApp CShub) — whitelist-gated onboarding with 2-step consent form; segmentation by hospital / specialty / level; personalized ZBS at every event touchpoint (announcement → registration → reminder → during → post-event survey); Content Hub with medical articles; digital engagement (quiz, AI e-card, loyalty points); 2-way Salesforce ↔ AdtimaBox data sync; QR code check-in at offline events; new-user acquisition at offline events (walk-in registration)

\- MCE (Multi-Channel Engagement) layer: campaign structured in 3 phases — Pre-launch (CPA), Launch (trialist prescription KPI), Post-launch (positive mentions KPI); channels = Zalo message + broadcast email + HCP portal content + 3rd party medical networks + webinar

\- Recommended: \*\*CShub Pro 2\*\* (Event Hub required) + \*\*Integration add-on (Salesforce 2-way sync)\*\*

\- Proof point: Full pipeline from zero database → whitelist-verified HCP community → personalized engagement tied to Salesforce CRM; offline events used as acquisition touchpoint for new HCPs

\- ⚠️ Salesforce integration is custom — requires tech confirmation and separate scoping

\*\*CS-13 — Global infant nutrition MNC, pregnant mom/new parent community, ZBS personalization by pregnancy week + location\*\*

\- Industry: F\&B / Nutrition — infant formula, baby nutrition

\- Target: B2C — pregnant mothers, new parents 25–40

\- Channel: Zalo media (lead form acquisition), direct OA search

\- Pain point: Cannot personalize communication without knowing pregnancy stage and location; content sent too early or too late loses relevance fast

\- Solution: Lead form collecting EDD (Expected Delivery Date) + location → system labels user by current pregnancy week + region → automated ZBS chain fires at each milestone week with stage-appropriate content (informative vs sale-trigger, depending on proximity to birth)

\- Label logic: `week of pregnancy` × `region (North/South)` × `objective (informative / sale trigger)` → each combination receives a different message

\- Recommended: \*\*CShub Pro 1\*\* (automation required for week-triggered ZBS journeys)

\- Proof point: Time-sensitive personalization at pregnancy-week granularity — right message at the right week, auto-triggered without manual campaign management

\*\*CS-14 — Global infant nutrition MNC, baby formula, UTC under-cap scan → points accumulation + redemption\*\*

\- Industry: F\&B / Nutrition — baby formula (infant/toddler stage)

\- Target: B2C — parents/caregivers buying baby formula at MT or pharmacy

\- Channel: MT, pharmacy; QR code under product cap (UTC scan-under-cap mechanic)

\- Pain point: MT channel blocks direct consumer data; brand needs to capture purchase behavior and build a CRM from formula buyers

\- Solution: Print UTC QR code under bottle cap → consumer buys, opens cap, scans QR on MiniApp → system validates code → accumulates points → redeems brand incentives (gifts, vouchers); follow-up ZBS messages based on point milestone to drive continued purchase and engagement

\- Recommended: \*\*CShub Pro 1\*\* + \*\*Campaign UTC 80M\*\*

\- Proof point: Under-cap UTC mechanic converts every formula purchase into a data point; follow-up ZBS sustains engagement across the baby's age stages
